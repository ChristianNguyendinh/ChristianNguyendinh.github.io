<b>Source:</b>

    <li>Sea salt</li>
</ul>

<hr />

<b>Time:</b> 

<ul>
    <li>5 M - Prep</li>
    <li>5 M - Cook</li>
</ul>

Basically a meal for babies. Probably the most low effort meal I know. But salmon tastes great.
